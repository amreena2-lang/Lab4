Welcome to UMBC IS Department

Welcome to the Department of Information Systems at UMBC. Our interdisciplinary programs equip students with the high-demand skills needed for success in IT and IS careers. Situated close to major technology hubs, our suburban campus provides an ideal environment for aspiring tech professionals. With multiple degree options, we offer flexible pathways for applying information systems across a range of industries. If you’d like to learn more, please reach out to us—we’re happy to answer your questions.
